Scones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup Almond flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder/li>
  • Pepperonni slices (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 °F.
  2. Microwave mozzarella and cream cheese for 45 seconds.
  3. Remove from microwave and add egg, almond flour, baking powder, Italian seasoning, and garlic. Mix well.
  4. Transfer dough onto a large piece of parchment paper and cover with another piece of parchment paper. Flatten out dough using a rolling pin to about ¼’’ thick. Remove parchment paper and shape with hands if desired.
  5. Transfer pizza onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove pizza and top with pepperoni slices or favorite toppings.
  7. Place pizza back in oven for another 5-8 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly.
